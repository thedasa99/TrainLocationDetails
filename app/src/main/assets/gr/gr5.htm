7. IF TRAIN STOPS AFTER PASSING THE STARTER IN OFF POSITION

BUT NOT PASSED THE ADVANCED STARTER AND FIRST FACING POINT ON THE ROUTE OF DESPATCH

 

 

SOUTH WESTERN RAILWAY

 

 

GUARD /LP to contact SM

And LP shall switch on flasher light on single line

 

 

SM to issue a Message +PN through

available means or as written advice

 

 

LP to record the same in RJB

 

 

Then only shall start the Train
