6. PROCEDURE FOLLOWED DURING SIGNAL PASSING AT ON WITHOUT AUTHORITY – SPAD

 

 

OCCASION

 

 

SOUTH WESTERN RAILWAY

 

PASSING HOME AT ON

 

Guard to inform SM

 

SM to inspect and record the Results

 

 

Signature of GLP obtained in Station Diary

 

AUTHORITY

 

Restarting Memo countersigned by Guard + Piloting

 

 

Any signal in advance shall be taken off

 

 

 

PASSING STARTER AT ON

 

GUARD TO INFORM SM

 

 

SM to inspect and record the Results

 

 

Signature of GLP obtained in Station Diary

 

 

AUTHORITY

 

Restarting Memo countersigned by Guard

 

Any signal in advance shall be taken off

 

 

Where necessary, Train may be backed

with Restarting Memo

 

 

 

PASSING ADVANCED STARTER AT ON

Guard to inform SM

SM to inspect and record the Results. Signature of GLP obtained in Station Diary

 

AUTHORITY

 

Restarting Memo countersigned by

Guard + PLCT

 

 

Where necessary, Train may be backed

with Restarting Memo
